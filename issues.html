Issues that matter to me

Quality municipal services: water, sewer, garbage, police, and fire

There are three bond propositions included on the November 8th general election ballot, which address all of these issues.

I have toured all the facilities and agree that improvements are necessary. Public tours will be offered and I encourage you all to take advantage of those opportunities.

Welfare of Citizens

I am an outdoors person and I walk wherever I can, but it is dangerous. We need sidewalks, bike lanes, and traffic laws to protect cyclists and pedestrians. Parks need to be improved and expanded where possible. Neighborhoods must be protected from random zoning changes. Families, and the homes they live in, are the lifeblood of this town.

Economic Development

We have a great town with wonderful attributes: a great environment, clean water, and an educated, productive workforce. Businesses are coming to Huntsville whether we court them or not. We do not have to sacrifice our community to bring in a business. Therefore, I do not support creation of an Economic Development Corporation funded with tax dollars.

I believe we must revise the Development Code to give better tools to manage the growth that is coming so we can help determine what this town will look like in the future.
