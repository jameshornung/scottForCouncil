My Vision and Goals

I believe it is the city’s responsibility to provide basic services at the lowest cost to help ensure the health and safety of its citizens. Therefore, my goals are to:

• Provide high quality basic services (water, sewer, and garbage) at the lowest possible cost.

• Protect citizens with first-rate police and fire departments.

• Safeguard property rights, and property values, through intelligent zoning.

• Promote the welfare of citizens with parks, bike lanes, and sidewalks.

• Keep taxes low and make housing affordable for the families of Huntsville.

Huntsville has much to offer: a beautiful environment, a rich history, and a productive, educated work force. People and businesses want to be here and I believe that providing reliable, basic services and protecting the city’s charm will draw more businesses to town. I support local businesses and entrepreneurs and do not believe it is necessary to bribe national chain stores with financial incentives to come to our city and take their profits out of town. I encourage them to come to town and compete on a level playing field with our established businesses.
